Admin Help Guide

Everything you need to know to manage your salon website effectively

Managing Services

Adding a New Service:

  1. Go to Services in the admin menu
  2. Click "Add Service"
  3. Fill in the service name and description
  4. Select a category (or create a new one)
  5. Set the price and duration
  6. Check "Is active" to make it visible on your website
  7. Click "Save"

Service Categories:

Categories help organize your services (e.g., "Hair Styling", "Skin Care", "Makeup"). Create categories first, then add services to them.

Managing Appointments

Viewing Appointments:

  1. Go to Appointments in the admin menu
  2. See all customer appointments in one place
  3. Use filters to find specific appointments
  4. Click on any appointment to view details

Appointment Status:

  • Pending: New appointment request
  • Confirmed: Appointment confirmed by staff
  • Completed: Service completed
  • Cancelled: Appointment cancelled

Managing Blog Posts

Creating a Blog Post:

  1. Go to Blog Posts in the admin menu
  2. Click "Add Blog Post"
  3. Enter a catchy title
  4. Write your content in the content area
  5. Add tags (e.g., "hair care", "beauty tips")
  6. Upload a featured image
  7. Set status to "Published" to make it live

Blog Tips:

  • Write helpful beauty tips and advice
  • Use high-quality images
  • Add relevant tags for better organization
  • Keep posts engaging and informative

Managing Team Members

Adding Team Members:

  1. Go to Team Members in the admin menu
  2. Click "Add Team Member"
  3. Enter their name and position
  4. Add their specialization (e.g., "Hair Styling", "Facial Specialist")
  5. Write a professional bio
  6. Upload a high-quality photo
  7. Check "Is active" to display them on your website

Customizing Your Website

Theme Settings:

  1. Go to Theme Settings in the admin menu
  2. Upload your salon logo
  3. Choose your brand colors
  4. Set the primary and secondary colors
  5. Save changes to update your website

Site Settings:

  1. Go to Site Settings in the admin menu
  2. Update your salon name
  3. Add your contact information
  4. Set your business hours
  5. Add social media links

Managing Gallery

Adding Gallery Images:

  1. Go to Gallery Images in the admin menu
  2. Click "Add Gallery Image"
  3. Upload high-quality photos of your work
  4. Add a descriptive title
  5. Write a brief description
  6. Check "Is featured" to highlight the image
  7. Check "Is active" to display it on your website

Managing Testimonials

Adding Testimonials:

  1. Go to Testimonials in the admin menu
  2. Click "Add Testimonial"
  3. Enter the client's name and profession
  4. Write their testimonial
  5. Set a star rating (1-5 stars)
  6. Check "Is featured" to highlight it
  7. Check "Is active" to display it on your website

Quick Tips:

  • Always check "Is active" to make content visible on your website
  • Use "Is featured" to highlight important content
  • Upload high-quality images for best results
  • Keep descriptions clear and engaging
  • Regularly update your content to keep your website fresh
